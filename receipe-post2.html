Pav Bhaji - Recipe



Pav bhaji is an Indian cuisine that originated in Mumbai. it's native to Mumbai and has now become popular in most metropolitan areas in India, especially in those of central and western Indian states like Gujarat and Karnataka. Pav origninates from the Portuguese Pao, which suggests bread. Bhaji in Marathi means vegetable dish.It consists of bhaji garnished with coriander, chopped onion, and a touch of lemon and lightly toasted pav. The pav is typically buttered on all sides.


Here is a simple and easy recipe for your mouth watering dish.


Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 4

01.

Firstly, in a large kadai heat 1 tbsp butter and add vegetables. cook and mash well.

02.

Now add 1 tsp chilli powder, 1/2 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander leaves.

03.

Heat a tbsp of butter and add 1/3 tsp chilli powder.Also add 1 tbsp coriander leaves, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 onion and one tsp lemon juice. saute well.

04.

Then add few drops(3 drops) of red food color in the mixture and mix it properly.

05.

Boil and mash for 5 minutes adjusting consistency.

06.

Finally, serve your hot and yummie dish with lemon and garnished onions,coriander
