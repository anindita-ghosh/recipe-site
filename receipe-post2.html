Pav Bhaji



Prep: 15 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 4

01.

Firstly, in a large kadai heat 1 tbsp butter and add vegetables. cook and mash well.

02.

Now add 1 tsp chilli powder, 1/4 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi and 2 tbsp coriander leaves.

03.

Heat a tbsp of butter and add 1/4 tsp chilli powder, 1/2 tsp pav bhaji masala, 1 tsp kasuri methi.Also add 1 tbsp coriander leaves, 1 tsp ginger garlic paste, 1 onion and 1/2 tsp lemon juice. saute well.

04.

Now add 3 drops of red food colour and mix well.

05.

Boil and mash for 5 minutes adjusting consistency.

06.

Finally, serve hot and yummie pav bhaji with lemon and garnished onions,coriander
