Fried Modak




Prep: 10 mins
Cook: 30 mins
Yields: 11 Modaks

01.

Firstly, in a large mixing bowl take 1½ cup maida, 2 tbsp rava and ½ tsp salt.Mix well and pour 2 tbsp hot ghee over the flour.

02.

Crumble and mix well making sure the flour is moist.Now add water as required and knead the dough for at least 5 minutes.

03.

Grease with oil, cover and rest for 20 minutes

04.

In a large pan heat 1 tsp ghee and add 1 cup coconut, 1/2 cup jaggery and saute for 5 minutes .The stuffing need to be sticky and aromatic.

05.

Now add 2 tbsp cashew, 2 tbsp almonds, 2 tbsp raisins, 1/2 tsp cardamom powder and mix well.
Stuffing is ready, keep aside to cool completely.

06.

Knead the dough slightly and pinch a small ball and flatten and start to roll using a rolling pin.Make sure you roll from sides and keep the centre slightly thick.

07.

Start pleating the edges slowly and gather everything.Press in middle and seal the modak forming a bundle.

08.

Deep fry in medium hot oil until modak turns golden and crisp.
Enjoy fried modak for 4-5 days when stored in an airtight container.
