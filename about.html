Who We are and What We do?

At Dish a Day, our mission is to help make healthy happen every day. We provide the information people need to make simple and quick recipes. We have been creating recipes and cookbooks for over 100 years.We are about moderation and balance-not strict rules or fad diets-because for healthy eating to become a way of life, it should be accessible, sustainable, inspiring and-above all-delicious.
